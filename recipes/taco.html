Taco

Description

A Mexican dish with meat, vegetables, and other toppings wrapped in a shell or flour/corn tortilla.

Ingredients

  • Taco Seasoning/Spices
  • Ground Beef
  • Olive Oil
  • Tomato Paste
  • Water
  • Toppings (Cheese, Lettuce, Sour Cream, whatever you want, etc.

Steps

  1. Put ground beef in skillet with olive oil
  2. Brown ground beef with olive oil and then drain the fat
  3. Add in spices, tomato paste, and water
  4. Bring to a simmer then cook until mixture thickens
  5. Place a portion of the meat into a soft shell or hard shell tortilla
  6. Add toppings of your choice
