Grilled Cheese

Description

A sandwich with a crispy exterior and a gooey center

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Put butter on one slice of bread
  2. On a hot skillet, place the bread butter side down
  3. Put your choice of cheese on top, then another slice of bread on top of that
  4. Cook until bottom is light brown, then flip the sandwich
  5. Take the sandwich off the skillet once the cheese is melted
