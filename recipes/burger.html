Burger

Description

Patty in between 2 buns, with other toppings in between

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Diced Onion
  • Garlic Cloves
  • BBQ Sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Buns
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients other than cheese and buns together
  2. Shape mixtrue into equal patties
  3. Grill for 2-4 minutes on each side
  4. For the last 2 minutes, add a slice of cheese on top and let it melt
  5. Place patty in between buns and add more BBQ Sauce
